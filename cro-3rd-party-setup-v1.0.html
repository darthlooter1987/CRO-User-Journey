What is Google Ads Conversion Tracking?
Plain-language explanation for business owners

Google Ads conversion tracking lets you see exactly which ad clicks lead to purchases on your landing pages. Without it, you're spending money on ads but can't tell which ones are actually working.

Why you need this
🎯
See which ads drive sales
Know exactly which campaigns, ad groups, and keywords lead to actual purchases
⚡
Optimize your ad spend
Google uses conversion data to show your ads to people most likely to buy
📈
Measure real ROI
Stop guessing — see your actual return on every dollar spent on Google Ads

Enter your Google Ads Conversion ID

Enter your Google Ads Conversion ID below. You can find this in your Google Ads account under Tools & Settings → Conversions.

Where do I find this?
  1. Log in to Google Ads
  2. Click Tools & Settings in the top menu
  3. Under Measurement, click Conversions
  4. Click your conversion action
  5. Copy the Conversion ID (starts with "AW-")

Troubleshooting ▼
Events not appearing
Events can take up to 24 hours to appear. Check that your Measurement ID is correct and the tag is installed on the right pages.
Duplicate events
This usually happens when the same tag is installed both directly and via Google Tag Manager. Remove one of the installations.
Conversion mismatch
If your conversion numbers don't match Google Analytics, check your attribution model settings and conversion counting method.
