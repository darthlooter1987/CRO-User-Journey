What element would you like to test? Choose from pre-defined categories or create a custom test. Each category explains why testing it matters.
Select a category and element to see guidance on why testing it matters and what results you can expect.
For MVT, we test combinations of elements. The system will automatically suggest strategic variation pairs based on your selection. Choose the element to test.
Hold Ctrl/Cmd to select multiple variations
The system suggests variation pairs based on industry best practices. You can select 2-4 variations. More variations = more data needed, but better insights.
How should traffic be split between versions? 50/50 gives you the fastest answer. Bandit is smarter — it sends more traffic to whoever's winning, protecting your revenue during the test.
50/50 split means each version gets exactly half the visitors. Bandit learns in real-time and shifts traffic to the winner, so you lose less revenue while testing.
Which metric should we measure? Pick the one that best reflects your goal. Each metric has different sample size requirements.
Conversion Rate is easiest to detect changes in (faster results). Revenue and LTV capture more business impact but need more data. Choose based on your business goal.
How certain do you want to be before we declare a winner? 95% confidence means there's only a 5% chance the result is a fluke. Higher confidence = more certain, but takes longer.
At 95% confidence, you're 95% sure the winner is truly better. Most teams use 95% as the standard. Only go to 99% if you're testing something with big business impact.
